About Us

Sigga Productions is a Johannesburg, Soweto - based film production company, established in 2021, founded by Sipho Zungu and Nomvuyo Zungu.

Our company consists of 4 powerful members, most of whom are award-winning film school graduates and all share a love for filmmaking and entertainment.

Our vision is to entertain, inspire and highlight(showcase) upcoming creatives today and in the future. The aim is to unleash greatness and excellence in everything we do.
Sigga Productions' mission is to produce creative content that excites and engages our audience.

We are a team of filmmakers with a passion for creating visually stunning and engaging content.
